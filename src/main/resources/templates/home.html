Welcome to YAET!

This site is born from the mix of a personal necessity and curiosity.

I am a software developer that was looking to learn the ins and outs of the trade in a nonwork environment. I wanted to be able to test, play and tweak with the tools of the trade without the pressures and requirements that come when developing for a client.

I was also looking for a tool to help me manage my finances. I had used some pay tools in the past, but I refused to pay for a glorified excel spreadsheet*. At the same time, the tools that I found online were not to my liking.

So in the end I decided to kill two birds with one stone and this is the result!
